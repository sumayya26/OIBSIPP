Exploratory Data Analysis on Retail Sales Dataset

Objective:
To explore sales, customers, and product trends and provide business insights.

Key Findings:
- Monthly and weekly sales trends identified.
- Top 10 products and customers highlighted.
- Correlation and seasonality observed in the data.

Recommendations:
1. Focus marketing before peak months.
2. Reward top customers for retention.
3. Maintain sufficient stock for best-selling items.
